# Employee Management System â€“ Requirements

1. The system shall store employee master data including code, name, email, phone, joining date, and status.
2. Each employee shall belong to one department at a time, with historical tracking.
3. Reporting hierarchy shall be modeled using employee-to-employee relationships with effective dates.
4. Employees may hold multiple roles over time, including a primary role.
5. Employee location assignments shall be tracked with history.
6. Compensation records shall be effective-dated and historical records shall never be overwritten.
7. Attendance shall record daily work type such as regular, overtime, WFH, or leave.
8. Leave requests shall support approval workflow and approver tracking.
9. Company assets shall be issued and returned with full audit history.
10. Performance reviews shall be conducted per review cycle with ratings and feedback.
11. Trainings and certifications shall be assigned, completed, and tracked.
12. Compliance documents shall be stored with signing and expiry details.
13. Employee separation shall record exit details and final settlement status.
14. System access shall be controlled using access groups with validity dates.
15. Employee skills shall be tracked with proficiency and experience.
16. Multiple employment types shall be supported, including contractors and vendor mapping.
